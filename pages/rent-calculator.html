What is Rent?

Rent is the periodic payment made by a tenant to a landlord in exchange for the use of property, such as an apartment or house.

The Renting Process

Renting involves finding a suitable property, signing a lease agreement, and making regular payments to the landlord.

Rent vs Buy

Renting offers flexibility and lower upfront costs, while buying builds equity and provides long-term stability.

Important Considerations When Renting

Consider location, budget, lease terms, and additional costs such as utilities and maintenance.

Ways to Reduce Rent Spending

Negotiate rent, consider roommates, move to a less expensive area, or downsize to a smaller space.
